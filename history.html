History of Video Games

Key Events in Video Game History

Year Event Description
1972 Pong Released One of the first arcade video games, developed by Atari.
1985 Nintendo Entertainment System (NES) Reinvigorated the home console market after the video game crash of 1983.
1994 Sony PlayStation Launch Introduced CD-ROM gaming and revolutionized 3D console graphics.
2001 Microsoft Xbox Release Entered the console market, bringing online multiplayer gaming to the mainstream.
2017 Breath of the Wild Award-winning open-world game that redefined the Zelda franchise.
